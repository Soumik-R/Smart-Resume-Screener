Full Stack Developer - Job Description

Position: Full Stack Developer
Experience Level: 3-5 Years
Location: Hybrid/Remote
Employment Type: Full-Time

Job Overview:
We are seeking a talented Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic engineering team. The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in both frontend and backend technologies, with a passion for building scalable, high-performance web applications.

Key Responsibilities:
• Design, develop, and maintain full-stack web applications using modern frameworks and technologies
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams including designers, product managers, and other developers
• Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code following best practices
• Implement responsive user interfaces with excellent user experience
• Develop and integrate RESTful APIs and microservices
• Optimize application performance and ensure scalability
• Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback
• Troubleshoot and debug applications across the full stack
• Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends

Required Technical Skills:

Frontend:
• Strong proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3
• Experience with modern JavaScript frameworks (React.js, Vue.js, or Angular)
• Knowledge of state management (Redux, Context API, Vuex)
• Understanding of responsive design and mobile-first development
• Experience with CSS preprocessors (SASS, LESS) or CSS-in-JS
• Familiarity with frontend build tools (Webpack, Vite, npm)

Backend:
• Proficiency in server-side languages (Node.js, Python, Java, or .NET)
• Experience with backend frameworks (Express.js, FastAPI, Django, Spring Boot)
• Strong understanding of RESTful API design and implementation
• Knowledge of authentication and authorization (JWT, OAuth)
• Experience with serverless architectures (AWS Lambda, Azure Functions) is a plus

Database:
• Proficiency in SQL databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server)
• Experience with NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Redis, DynamoDB)
• Understanding of database design, indexing, and optimization
• Knowledge of ORM frameworks (Sequelize, SQLAlchemy, Mongoose)

DevOps & Tools:
• Experience with version control systems (Git, GitHub, GitLab)
• Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI)
• Understanding of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)
• Knowledge of monitoring and logging tools (CloudWatch, ELK Stack)

Required Qualifications:
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)
• 3-5 years of professional experience in full-stack development
• Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
• Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
• Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities
• Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies

Preferred Skills:
• Experience with GraphQL and Apollo Client
• Knowledge of TypeScript
• Familiarity with testing frameworks (Jest, Mocha, Pytest, JUnit)
• Understanding of WebSockets and real-time applications
• Experience with AI/ML integration or LLM APIs
• Contributions to open-source projects
• Experience with design systems and component libraries
• Knowledge of web security best practices (OWASP)
• Experience with performance optimization and CDN
• Understanding of accessibility standards (WCAG)

• Professional development opportunities
• Collaborative and innovative work environment
• Latest tools and technologies
• Career growth opportunities

We are a fast-growing technology company focused on building innovative solutions that make a real impact. Our team values creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning. Join us to work on exciting projects with cutting-edge technologies.
